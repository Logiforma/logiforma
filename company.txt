Company Overview
Logiforma is a next-generation digital platform provider focused on transforming enterprise ERP ecosystems through intelligent automation, seamless integrations and data-driven insights. The company enables organizations to unlock the full potential of their ERP systems by creating a unified, scalable and intelligent digital backbone.
Logiforma serves as the operational backbone for industry leaders, providing a robust platform designed to drive digital transformation. The company addresses the common "bottlenecks" associated with enterprise resource planning by offering native, production-grade integrations that bypass brittle middleware and eliminate the need for custom glue code.
The platform is engineered for reliability and high performance, focusing on three core pillars:
	•	Connected Applications: Ensuring all software tools within an enterprise ecosystem communicate effectively.
	•	Intelligent Automation: Streamlining repetitive tasks to optimize human capital.
	•	Data Pipelines: Creating clean, reliable flows of information across the entire supply chain.

Company Background
Logiforma was established with the vision of addressing the growing complexity in enterprise ERP landscapes. As organizations increasingly rely on multiple systems, integrations and data sources, traditional ERP implementations often struggle to deliver real-time insights and operational agility.
Recognizing this gap, Logiforma developed a platform that acts as a centralized digital layer—integrating data, automating workflows and enabling intelligent reporting—thus redefining how enterprises manage their operations.
